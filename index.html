JUICY ROSE

Light bodied with sweet strawberry, honeydew and hints of rose pedal. Bright and aromatic with a long juicy delicious finish.

It is from this unique and picturesque hillside estate vineyard where the vines are positioned at the exact altitude with the perfect soils and optimal sun exposure. This Hillside vineyard then goes on to produce grapes known throughout the region by reputation for achieving the ripest and juiciest character possible. These grapes go on to be made into the delicious, succulently sweet and juicy wines from Juicy Hill.
